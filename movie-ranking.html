The best movies in my opinion

The top 3 movies of all time ranked in order of the best.

1.Interstellar

The most amazing sci-fi movie I've ever watched. This movie set my bar real high. It is going to be very difficult to prove me otherwise.

2.Shutter Island

Film directed by the same director as Interstellar. Absolutely mind bending film. Really messes with your brain and leaves you wanting for more.

3.Inside out

I know this one's surprising because it is a complete opposite genre of my first 2 picks. This is an animated movie that even adults are going to enjoy. Very feel good and heart warming. Guaranteed to leave you in tears
